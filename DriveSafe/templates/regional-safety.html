Regional Safety Recommendations

Stay informed about safety in the region you're traveling to. Here are some tips specific to certain areas:

Urban Areas

Stick to well-lit and populated streets, and avoid isolated areas at night.

Rural Areas

Know the nearest safe points or police stations. Roads may be less populated, so ensure your phone is charged.

Tourist Attractions

Beware of pickpockets in crowded areas. Keep your valuables secure and be mindful of your surroundings.

Always keep local emergency numbers saved on your phone in case of an emergency.
