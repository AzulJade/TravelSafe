Safety Tips

Your guide to staying safe while traveling.

Always Keep Your Belongings Secure

Always keep your belongings secure and within sight.

Use Reputable Taxi Services

Use reputable taxi services and verify the driver's identity.

Stay Aware of Your Surroundings

Stay aware of your surroundings, especially in crowded areas.

Trust Your Instincts

If something feels off, remove yourself from the situation.

Keep Emergency Contacts Handy

Keep emergency contacts saved on your phone for quick access.

Cash and Payments

Limit Cash Carrying

Limit the amount of cash you carry and use digital payment methods when possible.

Inform Someone About Your Plans

Inform someone about your travel plans and check in regularly.

Be Cautious

Be Cautious with Strangers

Be cautious when accepting help from strangers, especially in unfamiliar places.

Know Local Emergency Numbers

Familiarize yourself with local emergency numbers and procedures.

Have a Backup Plan

Always have a backup plan for transportation, especially at night.
